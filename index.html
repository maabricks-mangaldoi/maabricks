From Local Clay to Lasting Structures

Our meticulous manufacturing process ensures every brick meets the highest standards of quality, from sourcing raw materials to the final firing.

1

Clay Preparation & Sourcing

We carefully select and source high-quality clay from local deposits in the Mangaldoi region. The clay is then weathered, blended, and mixed to achieve the perfect consistency.

2

Hand Moulding & Shaping

Our skilled artisans hand-mould each brick using traditional stencils, ensuring a unique texture and authentic quality that machines cannot replicate. This time-honored process guarantees the classic look of our red bricks.

3

Drying Process

The 'green' bricks are carefully stacked in drying chambers to slowly remove moisture. This critical step prevents cracking and ensures structural integrity before firing.

4

Firing & Quality Control

Bricks are fired in a controlled kiln at temperatures up to 1200°C. This vitrifies the clay, giving the bricks their signature red color, strength, and durability. Rigorous quality checks are performed throughout.
